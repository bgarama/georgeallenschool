Who We Are

Located in Silala, Ganze Sub-County within Kilifi County, Kenya, George Allen School is dedicated to unlocking the limitless potential of every child. We believe that true education extends beyond the classroom walls, integrating academic discipline with civic responsibility and hands-on community engagement.

Our Pillars of Excellence

  • Academic Brilliance: Implementing thorough foundational studies to prepare students for advanced education pathways.
  • Community Empowerment: Partnering with local leaders, CBOs and initiatives to elevate regional livelihoods.
  • Character Development: Fostering integrity, resourcefulness, and leadership mindsets under our guiding motto: "The Sky Is The Limit".
