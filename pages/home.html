Our Story & Mission

Founded by our Director, Mtawali Mukutanno Zia, George Allen School was built from the ground up in Silala to serve and uplift the local community. Driven by a deep personal journey to address the educational needs of the area, the school was established step-by-step, transforming a local plot with gathered materials into a beacon of opportunity.

While operating as a dedicated private initiative, we thrive on the vital contributions and collaboration of our parents. Together, we do more than educate—we actively create local employment opportunities that directly benefit and strengthen our entire neighborhood.

Our Partners

We believe in collaborative growth. George Allen School works closely with local community stakeholders, organizations, and families to ensure our students have everything they need to succeed.
